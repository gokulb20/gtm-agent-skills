# SDR Qualification Handoff Template

```
=== QUICK QUALIFY (2 minutes) ===

Tier 1 signals (any 2 = Tier 1):
- [signal 1]
- [signal 2]
- [signal 3]

Tier 2 signals (any 2 = Tier 2):
- [signal 1]
- [signal 2]

Disqualify immediately if:
- [hard no #1]
- [hard no #2]
- [hard no #3]

Discovery questions (top 3):
1. [question that surfaces pain]
2. [question that confirms trigger]
3. [question that reveals buyer/champion]
```

## Design Principles

- Must be completable in under 2 minutes by a new SDR
- Tier 1 signals must be observable without a discovery call (firmographic + trigger)
- Hard disqualifiers must be checkable in <30 seconds
- Discovery questions designed to confirm tier assignment, not explore
