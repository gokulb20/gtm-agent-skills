# Watch-List Construction

## Tiering (from competitor-analysis)

| Tier | Monitoring intensity | Resources |
|---|---|---|
| Direct, top 3 | Heavy — daily alerts on website/pricing/hiring; weekly content; monthly deep-dive | Highest |
| Direct, others (4–6) | Medium — weekly check; monthly review | Medium |
| Substitute / status quo | Light — quarterly check; alert only if material shift | Low |
| Aspirational / adjacent | Light — quarterly check; alert if entering market | Low |

## Rules

- Maximum 8 competitors actively monitored
- Re-tier quarterly (Indirect → Direct, Direct → irrelevant)
- Add only when justified by recent deal interaction or ICP overlap
