# Weinberg's 19 Bullseye Channels

From *Traction* (Weinberg & Mares, 2015). Force consideration of every channel — the one that works is rarely the obvious one.

| # | Channel | Description |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Targeting blogs | Guest posts, niche blogs |
| 2 | Publicity | PR, press |
| 3 | Unconventional PR | Stunts, viral |
| 4 | Search engine marketing | SEM / paid search |
| 5 | Social and display ads | LinkedIn, Meta, display |
| 6 | Offline ads | Billboards, radio, print |
| 7 | Search engine optimization | SEO |
| 8 | Content marketing | Blogs, newsletters, long-form |
| 9 | Email marketing | Nurture sequences, drip |
| 10 | Engineering as marketing | Free tools, calculators, open-source |
| 11 | Viral marketing | Built-in virality, k-factor, refer-a-friend |
| 12 | Business development | Partnerships, integrations |
| 13 | Sales (outbound) | Cold email, cold call |
| 14 | Affiliate programs | Referral commissions |
| 15 | Existing platforms | App stores, Shopify app store, marketplaces |
| 16 | Trade shows | Industry conferences |
| 17 | Offline events | Meetups, dinners, small conferences |
| 18 | Speaking engagements | Podcasts, talks, panels |
| 19 | Community building | Slack, Discord, Reddit, forums |

## Stage-Appropriate Defaults

| Stage | Recommended channels |
|---|---|
| Pre-PMF | 1:1 outbound (sales), founder-led content, founder events, community |
| Early ($100k–$1M ARR) | Outbound at scale, niche communities, partnerships, content |
| Growth ($1M–$5M ARR) | Add: paid acquisition, broader content, podcast/speaking, BD |
| Scale ($5M+ ARR) | Add: brand spend, broad PR, events, large partnerships |
