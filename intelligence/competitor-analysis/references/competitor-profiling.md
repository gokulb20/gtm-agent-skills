# Competitor Profiling Guide

## Profile Minimums (Direct + Substitute)

Each competitor gets:
- One-line positioning (their words, from their site)
- Best-fit customer (size, segment, use case)
- 3 strengths (evidenced)
- 3 weaknesses (evidenced — G2 reviews are gold)
- Pricing (range + model)
- 1 recent move (<90 days)

No fabrication. If data is unavailable, write "Insufficient data — [what's needed]".

## Source Priority

Internal win/loss > customer interviews > G2/community > competitor materials > analyst reports.

## Pricing Triangulation

Pricing pages lie. Triangulate from:
- Vendr / Tropic public benchmarks (real negotiated ACVs)
- G2 / Reddit threads (customer-reported actual prices)
- Internal sales conversations (what buyers tell you the other quote was)

## Substitute Categories (force ≥1 each)

| Category | Examples |
|---|---|
| DIY / manual | Spreadsheets, email, internal tools, custom scripts |
| Hire a person | Agency, FTE, consultant, intern, VA |
| Do nothing | Status quo, low priority, "we'll deal with it later" |
| Shift budget elsewhere | "We'd spend $X on sales hiring / paid ads instead" |

The "shift budget" category is the most-missed alternative — it's what budget actually competes against in B2B.
