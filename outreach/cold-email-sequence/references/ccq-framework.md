# CCQ Copy Framework & Cliché Blocklist

## CCQ Framework — Context / Compliment-or-Connection / Question

The default copy structure for cold email. Inspired by Lavender (Will Allred + team) cold-email playbook.

**Structure:**
1. **Context** — Open with verified context (the personalization hook). Specific, recent, citable.
2. **Compliment-or-Connection** — Bridge with a specific observation or genuine connection to their situation.
3. **Question** — Close with one focused, low-friction question (the CTA).

Optimized for the 9-second / 25–50-word reading budget.

## Per-Touch Framework Assignment

| Touch | Default framework | Word target | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 — Opener | CCQ + Pain angle | 50–80 | Lead with verified hook; anchor to ICP pain |
| 2 — Follow-up #1 (D+3) | CCQ + Vision angle | 40–70 | Different value-prop facet from positioning |
| 3 — Follow-up #2 (D+7) | RTA (Resource-Then-Ask) | 50–80 | Share a useful resource; light ask |
| 4 — Mid-sequence pivot (D+11) | Compelling-Event reframe | 60–90 | Re-anchor on a time-sensitive trigger |
| 5 — Social-proof touch (D+15) | Outcome-based proof | 60–100 | Named customer with `[verified]` outcome |
| 6 — Break-up (D+19) | Permission-based close | 30–50 | "Seems like timing's off — close the loop?" |
| 7 (optional) — Resurrection (D+45) | Different hook/angle | 50–80 | Entirely different angle from original sequence |

## Opener Angles (from Bosworth + Sandler)

Three valid opener angles based on what the hook reveals:

- **Pain** — "You're hitting X problem" (from ICP P-T-O)
- **Power** — "You have the authority to fix Y"
- **Vision** — "You'd recognize the Z state"

Pick per recipient based on signals + role.

## Cliché Blocklist (HARD BAN)

These phrases are structurally prohibited — they trigger spam filters and signal "bulk":

- "I hope this finds you well"
- "I noticed" (without specific verified detail)
- "I came across your company"
- "we help X do Y" (generic value-prop opener)
- "reaching out to" / "touching base" / "circling back"
- "just checking in"
- "quick question"
- Multi-question CTAs (two+ asks in close)

## Buzzword Blocklist (HARD BAN)

Each costs ~3% reply rate. Replace with specifics:

- flexibility, visibility, scalable, strategic, leverage, synergy, robust, holistic, cutting-edge, best-in-class, world-class, seamless, end-to-end, next-generation, innovative

## Mobile-First Formatting Rules

- Subject ≤8 words (1–5 is sweet spot; 6–8 acceptable; >8 = bin)
- Preview ≤90 chars
- Body ≤125 words
- Opener ≤2 lines visible above the fold
- Paragraphs ≤4 lines
- Bullets when listing (3–5 max)
- One CTA only — "Worth a 15-min look?" beats "Book time on my calendar"
- Signature ≤3 lines

## Provenance Rules for Copy

| Provenance | Send behavior |
|---|---|
| `[user-provided]` or `[verified: <source>]` | Touch sends; pushes as `interaction:outreach` |
| `[unverified — needs check]` | Touch BLOCKED; pushes as `interaction:research` with `#unverified #review-required #needs-hook` |
| `[hypothetical]` | Never sends; never pushes. Local artifact only |
